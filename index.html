About Me

Hello! I'm Wenqi, a first year master student in the department of Human Centered Design and Engineering at University of Washington. Prior to my journey to Seattle, I received my bachelor degree in Information Management from Peking University.

I'm passionate about user experience research and design. I enjoy each step in the design process, from empathizing users to synthesizing ideas. I believe that human should come first in every design. I believe technology can change lives, and good human-centered design solutions can make a better world.

When I'm not working, I like travelling with friends and cooking delicious Chinese food. I love sharing joy and beauties in life through watercolor painting.
